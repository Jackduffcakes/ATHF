Diarrhea Summary

While foods are important in mediating diarrhea, the best remedy may be the medication Loperamide. One of the major issues that comes along with diarrhea is dehydration which is best dealt with with the World Health Organization formula rehydration salts- the most effective way to rehydrate. Additionally, a probiotic, specifically Lacrobacillus, helps treat and prevent diarrhea by providing friendly gut bacteria. The foods that are beneficial for diarrhea are banana flakes and applesauce because of their soluble fiber, pectin, and potassium while a food that is safe to eat when having diarrhea is white rice because it is easy to digest.

Other Important Information

The BRAT diet is a commonly used diet during diarrhea and stands for Bananas, Rice, Applesauce, Toast. While there is some value to this, as you see some of the foods we recommend include these, adhering to a restrictive diet particularly during diarrhea episodes can be dangerous (Duro and Duggan, 2007).

Psyllium, a soluble fiber supplement, can be beneficial for diarrhea as well mainly as a bulking agent.
