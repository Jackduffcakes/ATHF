Lactobacillus Friendly Bacteria to Help Diarrhea

About Lactobacillus Acidophilus

Lactobacillus has been used effectively to treat diarrhea and has proven particularly effective in conjunction with an oral rehydration solution (Guandalini et al., 2000), (Rautanen et al., 1998). Lactobacillus has even been demonstrated to have strong preventative capabilities for diarrhea (Black et al., 1989), (Szajewska et al., 2001).
