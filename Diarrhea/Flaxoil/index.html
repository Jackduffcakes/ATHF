Flaxseed Oil

About Flaxseed Oil

Flaxseed oil is unique in that it can help with both constipation and diarrhea. Flaxseed oil has "antidiarrheal activity" as well as antispasmodic activity or smooth muscle relaxation activity (Palla and Gilani, 2015; Palla et al., 2015). It contains a lot of Omega-3s as well which have have numerous health benefits.
Sources

(Palla et al., 2015), (Palla and Gilani, 2015)
