White Rice Easily digestable safe food

About White Rice

White rice is safe to eat when having indigestion. It is bland and non irritating. Many studies have used a rice based diet for people with diarrhea or digestion issues and it is widely used during bouts of diarrhea to not aggravate the digestive track or cause more diarrhea.
Sources

(Bhutta et al., 1991), (Gonlachanvit, 2010), (Triplehorn and Millard, 2002)
