Oats & Buckwheat

About Oats & Buckwheat

Oats and buckwheat are both effective cholesterol reducers. Buckwheat protein and oats Î²-glucan contribute to their cholesterol lowering effects as well as the cereal fibers in both. They are both healthy, whole grain energy sources.
