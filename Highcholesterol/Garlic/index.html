Garlic

About Garlic

Garlic can be an effective cholesterol reducer. It may help with high blood pressure as well (Ried et al., 2008). Boiled garlic may not be as effective in reducing cholesterol levels (Thomson et al., 2006).
Sources

(Adler and Holub, 1997), (Ali et al., 2000), (Durak et al., 2004), (Konjufca, Pesti, and Bakalli, 1997), (Kwon et al., 2003), (Warshafsky, Kamer, and Sivak, 1993), (Yeh and Liu, 2001)
