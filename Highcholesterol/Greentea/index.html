Green Tea

About Green Tea

Green tea's tea catechins and antioxidants help it reduce high cholesterol. One study suggests these effects are achieved through "lowering cholesterol synthesis and upregulating the hepatic LDL receptor" (Bursill, Abbey, and Roach, 2007). Green tea is well documented to lower LDL cholesterol levels.
Sources

(Bursill et al., 2001), (Kim et al., 2011), (Kono et al., 1992), (Koo and Noh, 2007), (Kuhn et al., 2004), (Maron et al., 2003), (Vinson and Dabbagh, 1998), (Yang and Koo, 1999), (Yokozawa,Nakagawa, and Kitani, 2002), (Zheng et al., 2011)
