Hawthorn Fruit

About Hawthorn Fruit

Hawthorn is the fruit and possibly even food with the strongest hypocholesterolemic abilities or abilities to lower cholesterol. It is packed with antioxidants and there is evidence it is beneficial for not just high cholesterol but for high blood pressure, heart disease, and for treating and preventing other cardiovascular diseases (Koch and Malek, 2011; Tassell et al., 2010).
