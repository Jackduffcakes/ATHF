Opuntia & Broccoli

About Opuntia & Broccoli

Opuntia cactus, or nopal, helps to control diabetes and provide internal protection by lowering "oxidation injury" (Budinsky et al., 2001). Broccoli is rich in many important nutrients including magnesium, ALA (alpha-lipoic acid), chromium, fiber, and vitamin C all of which can help prevent Type 2 diabetes.
