Spinach & Chicory

About Spinach & Chicory

Spinach is rich in many important nutrients including magnesium, ALA (alpha-lipoic acid), chromium, and fiber all of which can help prevent Type 2 diabetes. Chicory, or Cichorium intybus, has an antihyperglycemic and antidiabetic effect; it helps with diabetes treatment and symptom management.
