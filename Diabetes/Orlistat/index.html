Orlistat & Lactobacillus

About Orlistat & Lactobacillus

Orlistat is a clinically effective weight loss drug. It helps improve glycaemic control and improve and prevent type 2 diabetes. Lactobacillus is 'good bacteria' or probiotic. Gut microbial imbalance is a major contributor to type 1 diabetes. Poor gut bacteria is present in individuals with type 2 diabetes, too, and they can benefit from Lactobacillus.
