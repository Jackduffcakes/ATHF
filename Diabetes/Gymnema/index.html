Gymnema Sylvestre & Aloe

About Gymnema Sylvestre & Aloe

Gymnema Sylvestre and Aloe both help regulate blood sugar levels. Gymnema is also known as 'sugar destroyer' for its antidiabetic effects and effects on blood sugar (Thakur et al., 2012). Since they can lower blood sugar, monitor carefully if used in combination with antidiabetes drugs
Sources
