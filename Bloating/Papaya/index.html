Papaya Digestive aid

About Papaya

Papaya, specifically the digestive enzymes in papaya have been shown to help with digestive disorders (Muss, Mosgoeller, and Endler, 2012; Roxas, 2008). Digestive enzymes have also been shown to help IBS symptoms including "bloating, flatulence and abdominal pain" (Ciacci et al., 2011). Digestive enzymes have some potential interactions (mostly positive).
Sources

(Ciacci et al., 2011), (Muss, Mosgoeller, and Endler, 2012), (Roxas, 2008)
