Valerian, Chamomile, Hops

About Valerian, Chamomile, Hops

Valerian, chamomile, and hops are all safe to consume herbs. Individually they have positive effects on sleep but, particularly valerian and hops, together are much more effective. All three, valerian, hops, and chamomile have no or few possible interactions.
Sources

Valerian-Hops Combination: (Dimpfel and Suter, 2008), (Koetter et al., 2007), (Morin et al., 2005), (Müller et al., 2002)
Chamomile: (Abdullahzadeh and Naji, 2014), (Adib-Hajbaghery and Mousavi, 2017), (Chang and Chen, 2015)
