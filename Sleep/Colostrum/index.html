Colostrum & Eggs

About Dairy & Eggs

Colostrum and eggs are important sources of vitamin D, a nutrient that many people fall deficient in and that plays a major role in sleep. Not only that, colostrum and eggs are good sources of trytophan and glycine other important sleep nutrients. Colostrum is a nutrient rich substance that is beneficial for health in various ways.

Glycine: (Bannai and Kawai, 2012), (Chase, Soja, and Morales, 1989), (Inagawa et al., 2006), (Yamadera et al., 2007)
