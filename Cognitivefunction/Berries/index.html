Blueberries & Grape Juice

About Blueberries & Grape Juice

Blueberries and grape juice are packed with antioxidants and flavanoids. These specific antioxidants and fruit flavanoids significantly impact cognitive performance and are especially important for reducing cognitive decline. Blueberries have more cognitive promoting nutrients than grape juice, but both help.
