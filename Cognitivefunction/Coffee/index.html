Coffee or NeuroMaster

About Coffee & NeuroMaster

Coffee provides cognitive enhancing antioxidants and chlorogenic acid. Many processed coffee brands have varying amounts of chlorogenic acid and have toxins (Batista et al., 2003; Mills et al., 2013; Tozlovanu and Pfohl-Leszkowicz, 2010). Some of coffee's beneficial effects may be from the caffeine in coffee. NeuroMaster is coffee fruit extract and provides the beneficial effects of coffee in capsule form.
