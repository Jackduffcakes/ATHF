Spinach

About Spinach

Spinach has many different nutrients that help cognitive function. Spinach has lutein, vitamin C, betacarotene, polyphenols, vitamin A, iron, fiber, nitrates, B2, and choline all of which contribute to cognitive function. Spinach is one of the best sources of lutein which is an effective cognitive enhancing carotenoid.
