Octane Oil

About Octane Oil

Octane Oil is MCT Oil, but specifically the C-8 MCTs which provide more energy. MCT or medium-chain triglyceride oil is an energy source that does not deposit as fat. It provides an alternative energy source other than glucose for the brain, is converted to energy quickly, increases omega-3s in the brain, and can improve memory.
