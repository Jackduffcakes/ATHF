Avocados

About Avocados

Avocados have fiber, folate, vitamin b6, vitamin c, and lutein all of which help cognition. Studies have even specifically linked avocados to cognitive improvement (Ameer, 2016; Johnson et al., 2015; Scott et al., 2017).
Sources

(Ameer, 2016), (Bryan and Calvaresi, 2004), (Huskisson, Maggini, and Ruf, 2007), (Johnson et al., 2015), (Renzi-Hammond et al., 2017), (Scott et al., 2017), (Solfrizzi, Panza, and Capurso, 2003), (Vercambre et al., 2009), (Wengreen et al., 2007)
