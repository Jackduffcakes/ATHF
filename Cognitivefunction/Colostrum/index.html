Colostrum & Eggs

About Colostrum & Eggs

Bovine colostrum is the nutrient rich substance that cows secrete shortly after giving birth. It contains vitamins A, B2, B9, B12, and D as well as immune boosting nutrients like IgG, Cytokines, and Lactoferrin. There is evidence that colostrum can improve Alzheimer's symptoms (Buhmeyer). Eggs are, also, rich in many nutrients beneficial for brain and overall health.
