Turmeric

About Turmeric

Turmeric is the main spice used in curry. It has a lot of antioxidants and various health benefits one of which being cognitive enhancement. Turmeric, particularly the curcumin in turmeric, helps to prevent and mediate cognitive impairment. As an herb it has minor possible interactions.
