Flaxseed A Superfood For Cognitive Function

About Flaxseed

Flaxseed is packed with nutrients. It has omega-3s, choline, fiber, vitamin E, antioxidants, magnesium, and b1 all of which help cognitive function. Many of the nutrients in flaxseed are important for preserving cognitive function and reducing risk of cognitive impairing diseases.
