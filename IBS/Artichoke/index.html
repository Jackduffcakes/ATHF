Artichoke Extract Herbal remedy to IBS

About Artichoke Extract

Artichoke extract or Cynara scolymus has proven to be a safe and side effect free herb for IBS (no interactions). The flavonoids and caffeoylquinic acids in artichoke extract are what seem to aid IBS. Artichoke extract is "demonstrated to have both preventive and curative roles in IBS" (Rahimi and Abdollahi, 2012). There is some evidence that it helps the growth of beneficial gut bacteria as well (Costabile et al., 2010).
Sources

(Costabile et al., 2010), (Giacosa et al., 2015), (Rahimi and Abdollahi, 2012), (Wegener and Fintelmann, 1999)
