Sexual Health & Dysfunction Summary

The three important nutrients for sexual health are Vitamin E, selenium, and zinc- all found in pumpkin seeds. These three nutrients benefit libido, semen quality, reproductive performance and more (this explains why foods like oysters, very high in zinc, are often seen as aphrodisiacs). The other nutrients that benefit sexual function are antioxidants and nitrates found in pomegranate and beet juice; these antioxidants and nitrates help endothelial function and, so, help increase blood flow to the genitalia. The herb, maca, also has strong sexual enhancing properties and can help increase libido and sexual health for both men and women.
