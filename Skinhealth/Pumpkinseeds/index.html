Pumpkin Seeds & Spinach

About Pumpkin Seeds & Spinach

Pumpkin seeds are rich in important nutrients for skin health and acne reduction- vitamin E, selenium, and zinc; spinach is rich in vitamin E and zinc as well but also vitamin A, carotenoids and lutein which benefit skin health, too.
