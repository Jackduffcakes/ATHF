About Fish Oil

The omega-3s in fish oil have strong anti-inflammatory properties. Acne being fairly widespread nowadays may be attributable to the 'western diet' which is high in the glycemic index and very low in omega-3s (Logan, 2003). Omega 3 has even been used successfuly for acne treatment (Yoon et al., 2014).
