Cherry Juice

About Tart Cherry Juice

Tart cherry juice reduces muscle damage and attenuates soreness. The antioxidants and anti-inflammatory chemicals like anthocyanin in cherry juice make cherry juice a particularly effective aid in recovery for high intensity athletes, and there is some evidence that cherry juice can increase endurance performance as well (Levers et al., 2016; Yarahmadi et al., 2014).
Sources
