Beet Juice

About Beet Juice

Beet juice reduces the oxygen cost of exercise; the nitrates in beet juice lower athletes' vo2 and thus raises their vo2 max. Its benefit for endurance athletes is well evidenced and there is some evidence that it also improves sprinting performance (Kandiah, 2017; Thompson et al., 2015; Thompson et al., 2016).
Sources
