Colostrum

About Colostrum

Bovine colostrum is the nutrient rich substance that cows secrete shortly after giving birth. Colostrum supplementation has been shown to enhance athletic performance and recovery for both high intensity athletes and endurance athletes (with no interactions). Not only that, colostrum has vitamin D which athletes are frequently deficient in; vitamin D deficiency harms athletic performance for many athletes particularly indoor athletes.
