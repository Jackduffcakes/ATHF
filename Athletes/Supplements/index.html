About Supplements

Vitamin D deficiency is surprisingly common among athletes particularly during winter time and for indoor athletes. Iron deficiency is prevalent among endurance athletes with female athletes being most susceptible; female athletes are also particularly susceptible to calcium deficiency. Magnesium is another nutrient many athletes lack in. Deficiencies in these nutrients harms athletic performance.
