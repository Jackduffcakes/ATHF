About Colostrum & Dairy

Bovine colostrum is the nutrient rich substance that cows secrete shortly after giving birth and has many health benefits. Colostrum and dairy is high in casein protein which is a protein that lasts longer; some body builders like to eat cottage cheese before sleeping because of its casein content. Cottage cheese and dairy seem to be the healthiest dairy option because of their high nutritional content particularly high protein content.
