Vitamin B6

About B6

Supplementation of vitamin B6 is an effective treatment for nausea and vomiting. Several studies compare the efficacy of B6 with ginger in treating nausea and find both effective in treating nausea (Niebyl and Goodwin, 2006; Smith et al., 2004; Sripramote and Lekhyananda, 2003).
