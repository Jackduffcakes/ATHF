Lemon & Chamomile

About Lemon & Chamomile

Lemon juice and even lemon scent have both help reduce nausea (Aliasl et al., 2015; Kia et al., 2014). Chamomile, a medicinal herb with many different benefits, helps reduce nausea as well (Bischoff and Renzer, 2006; Sanaati et al., 2016). Since these are easily and frequently combined in tea, we grouped them together.
Sources

(Aliasl et al., 2015), (Bischoff and Renzer, 2006), (Kia et al., 2014), (Sanaati et al., 2016)
