About Flaxseed

Flaxseed has a lot of fiber and nutrients. Flaxseed, especially flaxseed oil, has been shown to "exhibit laxative activity" and flaxseed can help relax smooth muscle (Palla and Gilani, 2015; Palla et al., 2015).
