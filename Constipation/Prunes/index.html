Prunes One of The Best Foods for Constipation

About Prunes

Prunes are one of the most cited foods for constipation treatment. Their high content of dietary fiber, specifically insoluble fiber, is credited for their laxative effect. They are safe and palatable and "should be considered as a first line therapy" (Attaluri et al., 2011).
Sources
