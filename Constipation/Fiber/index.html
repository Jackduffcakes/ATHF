Fiber The Key Nutrient For Constipation

About Fiber

Psyllium is soluble dietary fiber and has few potential interactions. Psyllium has been shown specifically to help with constipation (McRorie et al., 1998; Mehmood et al., 2011). Psyllium is not the only option, though; there is extensive evidence that dietary fiber can both prevent and help manage constipation.
