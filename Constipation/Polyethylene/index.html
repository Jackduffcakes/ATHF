Polyethylene Glycol Extensively studied laxative

About Polyethylene Glycol

Countless studies have proven the efficacy of polyethylene glycol in treatment of diarrhea. It has been found to be palatable, safe, and without significant side effects. It is even more effective or better accepted than other treatments like lactulose or milk of magnesia (Attar et al., 1999; Loening-Baucke and Pashankar, 2006).
