St John's Wort & Turmeric

About St John's Wort & Turmeric

St John's Wort is as effective as many antidepressants and has fewer side effects. There are possible interactions causing serotonin syndrome if taken with antidepressants. Turmeric exerts both antianxiety and antidepressive effects along with its strong antiinflammatory effects.
