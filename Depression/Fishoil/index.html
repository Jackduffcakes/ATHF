Fish Oil

About Fish Oil

The omega-3s DHA and EPA which are particularly important for mental health are in fish oil. Fish oil is important in the prevention and treatment of depression and mental health issues and, also, provides numerous other health benefits. Omega-3s have few possible interactions.
