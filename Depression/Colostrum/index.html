Colostrum

About Colostrum, Vitamin D, Eggs

Bovine colostrum is the nutrient rich substance that cows secrete shortly after giving birth. It contains vitamins A, B2, B9, B12, and D as well as immune boosting nutrients like IgG, Cytokines, and Lactoferrin. Vitamin D deficiency is common and vitamin D contributes to mental health. Eggs are, also, rich in many nutrients beneficial for mental and overall health.
