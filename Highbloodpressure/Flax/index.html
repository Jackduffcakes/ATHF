Flaxseed A Superfood To Help Reduce Blood Pressure

About Flaxseed

Flaxseed and flaxseed oil has a lot of omega-3s which have been linked to blood pressure reduction. One serving of flaxseeds has 51mg magnesium and 2.9g omega-3s. Flaxseed oil has even more omega-3s.
Sources

(Caligiuri et al., 2014), (Khalesi, Irwin and Schubert, 2015), (Nestel et al., 1997), (Paschos et al., 2007), (Ursoniu et al., 2015)
