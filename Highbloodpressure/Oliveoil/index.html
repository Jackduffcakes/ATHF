Olive Oil & Fish Oil

About Olive Oil & Fish Oil

Olive oil's polyphenols and high oleic acid content make it particularly effective at lowering blood pressure. It is a great alternative to butter, salad dressing, or other vegetable oils. Fish Oil's omega-3s, particularly DHA, make it one of the best supplements to help lower blood pressure and increase cardiovascular health.
