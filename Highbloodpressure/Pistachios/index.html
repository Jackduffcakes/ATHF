Pistachios Help Reduce Blood Pressure

About Pistachios

Pistachios reduce peripheral blood vessel constriction or in other terms they help lower systolic blood pressure. Pistachios are rich in potassium and are fairly rich in dietary fiber and magnesium as well.
Sources

(Dreher, 2012), (Mohammadifard et al., 2015), (Parham et al., 2014), (Sauder et al., 2014), (West et al., 2012)
