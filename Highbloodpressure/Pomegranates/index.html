Pomegranates Help Reduce Blood Pressure

About Pomegranates

Pomegranate has antioxidants and tannins both shown to reduce blood pressure. It also has a ton of potassium which plays a major role in blood pressure regulation. Many studies have demonstrated the effectiveness of pomegranates in lowering blood pressure.
Sources

(Asgary et al., 2013), (Asgary et al., 2014), (Aviram and Dornfeld, 2001), (Aviram et al., 2004), (Basu and Penuganda, 2008), (Tsang et al., 2012)
