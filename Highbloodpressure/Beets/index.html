Beet Juice & Pomegranate

About Beet Juice & Pomegranate

Beets have a lot of nitrates which lower blood pressure. Studies have shown beets and beet juice to lower blood pressure even the same day as consumption (Hobbs et al., 2012). Pomegranate has antioxidants, tannins and potassium shown to reduce blood pressure.
Sources

Beet Juice: (Berry et al., 2014), (Coles and Clifton, 2012), (Eggebeen et al., 2013), (Hobbs et al., 2012), (Hobbs et al., 2013), (Kapil et al., 2010), (Kelly et al., 2013), (Siervo et al., 2013), (Sobko et al., 2009), (Vanhatalo et al., 2010), (Webb et al., 2008)
Pomegranate: (Asgary et al., 2013), (Asgary et al., 2014), (Aviram and Dornfeld, 2001), (Aviram et al., 2004), (Basu and Penuganda, 2008), (Tsang et al., 2012)
