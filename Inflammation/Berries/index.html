Blueberries

About Blueberries

Blueberries are rich in fruit flavanoids, anti-inflammatory phytochemicals, particularly anthocyanin, resveratrol, and quercetin. These specific flavanoids have strong anti-inflammatory effects and blueberries are, also, rich in beneficial antioxidants.
