Boswellia Serrata & Ginger

About Boswellia & Ginger

Boswellia serrata and ginger are both anti-inflammatory herbs. Both of these herbs seem to have other health benefits, as well, and there is strong evidence for their anti-inflammatory effects particularly for the boswellic acids in boswellia. As herbs, ginger has minor possible interactions, and Boswellia has none.
