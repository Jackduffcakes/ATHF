Spinach

About Spinach

Spinach is rich in magnesium; magnesium deficiency is a major cause of inflammation and magnesium can alleviate inflammation. Spinach, also, has anti-inflammatory quercetin, as well as vitamin E which may help alleviate inflammation or deficiency in may contribute to inflammation.
