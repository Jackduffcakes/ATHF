Fish Oil

About Fish Oil

Fish oil has anti-inflammatory omega-3s specifically EPA and DHA. Omega-3s are crucial in lowering inflammation and they counteract the inflammatory effects of a high omega-6 diet (Other Important Information). Fish oil is one of the best sources of omega-3s especially because it contains DHA and EPA which are usually the omega-3s people stuggle to get enough of.
