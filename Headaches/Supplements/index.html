Supplements

About Supplements

Magnesium, omega-3s (fish oil), Q10, and riboflavin are the most important nutrients to get. Calcium, Vitamin D, and potassium may help as well or a deficiency may be contributing to the headaches.
Sources

Omega-3s: (Bouwens et al., 2009), (Harel et al., 2002), (Mccarty, 1996), (Tajmirriahi et al., 2012), (Wall et al., 2010)
Q10 (CoQ10): (Cordero et al., 2012), (Gaul, Diener, and Danesch, 2015), (Hershey et al., 2007), (Mancuso et al., 2010), (Rozen et al., 2002), (Sándor et al., 2005)
Riboflavin: (Boehnke et al., 2004), (Condò et al., 2009), (Maizels, Blumenfeld, and Burchette, 2004), (Schoenen, Jacquy, and Lenaerts, 1998)
Potassium, Vitamin D, Calcium, Antioxidants: (Kubitzek et al., 2003), (Tall et al., 2004), (Thys-Jacobs, 1994), (Wang et al., 1999)
