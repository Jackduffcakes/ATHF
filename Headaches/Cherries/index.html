Tart Cherries

About Cherries

Cherries have a lot of potassium and some magnesium and calcium as well. Potassium has been linked tenuously to headache and migraine reduction. More importantly cherries have antioxidants cyanidin and anthocyanin which are anti-inflammatory and can have a pain reducing effect.
Sources

(Kubitzek et al., 2003), (Ou et al., 2012), (Seeram et al., 2004), (Tall et al., 2004), (Wang et al., 1999)
