Headaches & Migraines Summary

The important nutrients for headaches and migraines are magnesium, omega-3s, Q10, and riboflavin with potassium, vitamin D, calcium, and anti-inflammatory antioxidants being of benefit as well. Two herbs, feverfew and ginger, have proven very effective as prophylactic, or preventitive, treatment options particularly in combination. These herbs and nutrients (particularly magnesium) have been used as active treatments, too.
